# Content Policy

This app is designed to store and present scientific metadata in a source-first way.

## Default policy
- Store paper metadata for up to 365 days.
- Store generated summaries for up to 365 days.
- Store raw source snapshots for a short diagnostic window of 30 days.
- Avoid long-term storage of publisher-supplied raw abstracts unless the source is clearly open access and reuse-permissive.
- Always provide direct links back to PubMed and, where present, the DOI landing page.

## Product rules
- AI summaries are editorial aids, not scientific or medical advice.
- AI-generated illustrations are decorative and must not resemble diagnostic imagery, charts, or article figures.
- The product should prefer omission over misleading enrichment when a paper lacks enough context for a safe summary or image.
